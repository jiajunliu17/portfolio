About Me

I love data analytics.

Ten years ago, I thought my dream was to be a university professor, teaching students, publishing papers, and jabbing mice tail veins. A Ph.D. degree seemed to be perfect for that dream. I then spent eight years in labs earning my degree and working in various roles in China and the US.

After analyzing tons of experimental data in labs, I cultivated a keen interest in data analytics. While finding insights and telling stories from data, I feel like my skills are not limited to academia and would like to apply them to practical business problems. Therefore I enrolled in the MBA program and specialized in Business Intelligence & Data Analytics (BIDA). I tried my best to add 'business flavor' to my data skills and utilize them in each project.

My specialties include problem-solving, critical thinking, business storytelling, and data visualization. So far, I have R and Python programming, SQL, SSMS, Power BI, Tableau, and Google Analytics in my data tool kit. I'm also a beginner in machine learning. I will stay excited to learn other programming languages and analytics tools.
